Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is sacred to us. This policy explains how Innerlight collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address and encrypted password when you register
  • Conversation Data: Messages you share during conversations
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our emotional support services
  • Personalize your experience based on past conversations
  • Send important service updates (never marketing spam)
  • Ensure the safety and security of our platform

Data Protection

Your conversations are encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication via Supabase
  • Regular security audits
  • Strict access controls for our team

Data Retention

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database (GDPR compliant)
  • Anthropic Claude: AI conversation processing
  • n8n: Workflow automation (self-hosted)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Cookies

We use essential cookies only for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or share data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Innerlight is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@innerlight.app
